Corbett Safari Zones

Jim Corbett National Park is divided into Eight safari zones for wildlife exploration and observation.

The Dhikala Zone

    Dhikala Zone is one of the most visited Safari zone of the Corbett National Park.... Located 50 kms inside the dense forest of Jim Corbett.

  • Entry Gate:- Dhangadi Gate
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

The Bijrani Zone

    Bijrani Range is located close to Amdanda Gate anear Ramnagar.... Visitors can have higher probablity of tiger sighting during Bijrani Safari.

  • Entry Gate:- Amdanda Gate
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

The Jhirna Zone

    Jhirna zone is known as easy and best wildlife sightseeing.....Blessed with amazing wildlife beauty, Jhirna zone is open all round the year for visitors.

  • Entry Gate:- Jhirna Gate or khar Gate
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

Sitabani Forest Zone

    Sitabani Forest Reserve is located in Corbett National Park(18 km from Ramnagar barage gate in Amgarhi)....The main gate of Sitabani Forest Reserve is at Bhandarpani village.

  • Entry Gate:- Bhandarpani Village
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

Garjia Zone

    Garjia Tourist Zone is a very popular area of the spectacular Corbett Tiger Reserve.... It is,situated at a suitable distance from Ramnagar & ,it is the most preferred zone for Wildlife enthusiasts visiting the park for day visit.

  • Entry Gate:- Ringora Village
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

Phato Zone

    Phato Zone is one of the farthest zones from Ramnagar, as it’s more than 20km away from the main city....While the giant Tumdiya Dam on the way to Phato zone opens the scope of boating at the dam.

  • Entry Gate:- Mohan-Nagar,Maldhan
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

The Durga Devi Zone

    Durga Devi Zone is the hill safari zone filled with wildlife beauty and adventure within the Corbett National Park....Durga Devi Zone is open for visitors 15 October to 15 June and closed for monsoon.

  • Entry Gate:- Mohan-Nagar,Maldhan
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •

The Dhela Zone

    Dhela Ecotourism zone remains open for tourists throughout the year....The zone spreads over 1,173 hectares, which has mixed forests of Sal, Rohini, Haldu, Bahera, Kusum vegetation, and tigers, leopards, Asiatic elephants, sloth bears and king cobras.

  • Entry Gate:- Dhela Village
  • Winter safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-07:00am-10:00am
    Evening Time-02:00pm-5:30pm
  • Summer safari Timings:-
    Morning Time-06:00am-9:30am
    Evening Time-03:00pm-6:30pm
    •
